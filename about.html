About Me

I’m Bryant “Colby” Hood — a strategic finance leader and builder who sits at the intersection of finance operations, automation, and AI. For over a decade, I’ve helped large retail and technology organizations (Amazon, Capital One, Lowe’s, Academy Sports + Outdoors) turn complex operational problems into scalable, data-driven systems.

At Amazon, I lead Finance Operations for Retail Accounts Receivable, managing a $40B contra-COGS revenue stream and building AI-driven automation that replaces hundreds of manual processes. My work blends technical execution with controllership: designing automation roadmaps, scaling self-service analytics, and using AI to enhance everything from collections strategy to cross-business financial modeling.

Before Amazon, I built the foundation for large-scale financial automation programs at Capital One and Lowe’s — from AWS cost-optimization saving $40M+ to developing Power BI dashboards that replaced hundreds of hours of manual FP&A effort.
