Background

I’m a Finance Operations Manager at Amazon overseeing automation programs that manage billions in revenue and contra-COGS flows. My focus is on bridging finance, analytics, and tech — translating financial complexity into streamlined, data-driven systems.

Before Amazon, I worked across corporate finance and retail operations at Academy Sports + Outdoors and Lowe’s. Over time, I’ve grown obsessed with controllership, process optimization, and AI-assisted decisioning.

Focus Areas

  • AI and RPA integration for finance workflows (UiPath, n8n, Python)
  • Analytics and visualization (Power Query, DAX, Quicksight)
  • Automation of AR/AP processes and data pipelines
  • Building internal GPT assistants for FinOps and business teams

Philosophy

“Automation isn’t about replacing people — it’s about freeing them from repetition so they can actually think.”

My approach is pragmatic: simplify data flows, build scalable logic, and automate where it matters. If it saves time, reduces risk, or increases insight, it’s worth building.
